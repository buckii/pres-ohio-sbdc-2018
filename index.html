Top 10 Tips

for
Efficient, Effective Small Business Websites

1. Have a Goal

Common website goals

  • E-commerce - sell products or services online
  • Lead generation - collect information from potential customers
  • Advertising - grow traffic/visitors to view revenue-generating ads
  • Membership/Community - add active members who will contribute to or use your site's content
  • Affiliate - advertise and generate sales for other companies' products (earning a % of sales)
  • Informational/Marketing - inform visitors about your products and services; build credibility; nurture leads

Common website goals

  Key metric(s) Difficulty
E-commerce Sales $ Moderate
Lead Generation # of Leads Easy
Advertising Clicks, Impressions, Ad Revenue Difficult
Membership/Community # of Members, # of Visits Moderate
Affiliate # of Clicks, Aff. Revenue Easy
Info/Marketing # of Leads, Engagement (time, pages), Likes, Followers Easy

3. Get Professional Help... or Don't

Deciding if you need help

  • In-house web design and development skillsets
  • Urgency
  • Budget availability
  • Acceptable quality level
  • Cost of a slow or low-quality website

4. Use Great Tools

What is a Content Management System (CMS)?

Wikipedia says...
A web content management system (WCMS) is a software system that provides website authoring, collaboration, and administration tools designed to allow users with little knowledge of web programming languages or markup languages to create and manage website content with relative ease.

Why you need a CMS

  • You can update your own website if it is built on a CMS!
  • Low Cost to build, host and maintain
  • Easy Customization via settings, plugins and themes
  • Thorough Documentation from the user and developer communities
  • Ease of Use for administrators (you)
  • Workflow Management for content approval, scheduling and publishing
  • Availability of low-cost support/development

CMS/Platform Options

  • WordPress
  • Wix
  • Weebly
  • Squarespace
  • Drupal
  • Shopify (e-commerce)

Other Helpful Tools

  • Canva - image editing
  • Pingdom Tools - website performance testing
  • Google mobile speed test - mobile speed optimization
  • MailChimp - email campaign management
  • HubSpot Website Grader - SEO, social, and more
  • WebAIM Wave - Accessibility testing

5. Refine Your Message

Is Search Engine Optimization Important?

  • Know where you find your best clients/prospects
  • Speed Dating vs. Matchmaker vs. College
  • Align your goals with Google's goals

6. Photos Are Important

Plan for your photo and video needs

  • Professional headshots
  • Environmental photos/video
  • 360 Virtual Tour
  • Brand Video
  • Testimonials

7. Measure

Best analytics options

  • Google Analytics - website metrics
  • Google Search Console - SEO visibility diagnostics
  • FullStory - visitor recordings
  • Facebook Business Insights

8. Don't Buy the Cheapest Hosting

Shared vs. Virtual Private Server

  • Share Hosting
    • Lowest cost
    • Performance affected by other sites
  • Virtual Private Server (VPS)
    • Moderate cost
    • Dedicated resource mean consistent performance
    • More freedom and control of your site

Comparing popular hosting options

  • WordPress.com
  • GoDaddy
  • DreamHost
  • Bluehost
  • Amazon Web Services - Lightsail

9. Invest for the Long-Term

A great website is never finished

  • Consider maintenance costs
  • Update content regularly
  • Photos become outdated quickly
  • Your site can always be faster and more thorough

10. Refresh Every 3 Years

Your business is either growing or dying.

So is your website.
